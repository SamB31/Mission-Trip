About The Mission


  Project Juembo is a mission that was started by the Chinchen family in a hope to supply various school supplies and a few goodies to the children of Malawi. This mission has been going on for many years, and provides the children with essentials that are packed in a backpack that they get to call their own. A few examples of what is included in the backpacks are pencils, socks, a pack of crayons and markers, and a notebook. This allows them to go to school with everything they will need to help strengthen their learning experience. There is a secondary reason I am going on this mission trip.
